Predicting Diseases Before they Happen

Project Info:

We have been treating people in response for decades. That sucks! By looking at your personal genetic data, SNPs specifically, and some demographic data, we can predict your risk of getting a complex disease (cancer, diabetes...). Currently, Polygenic risk scoring is used, but new advancements make machine learning look much more promosing.
